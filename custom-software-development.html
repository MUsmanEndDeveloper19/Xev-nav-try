Custom Software Development Questions Answered

What's the difference between custom software and off-the-shelf solutions?

Off-the-shelf software offers generic features built for a broad market, while custom software is designed specifically around your workflows, data, and business logic — giving you full ownership, flexibility, and a competitive edge that pre-built tools can't match.

How long does a custom software project take?

Timeline depends on scope — a focused MVP typically takes 6 to 10 weeks, while full enterprise platforms range from 3 to 6 months. We provide a detailed timeline and milestone plan after the discovery phase.

Can you integrate with our existing systems and APIs?

Yes. We regularly build custom integrations with CRMs, ERPs, payment gateways, and internal databases, connecting your new software seamlessly with the tools you already use.

Do we need technical expertise to manage the software after launch?

No. We build intuitive admin panels and documentation so your team can manage day-to-day operations without technical expertise, and we offer ongoing support retainers for anything beyond that.

Is our data and source code secure with your team?

Absolutely. We sign NDAs before any project discussion, follow security-first development practices, and transfer full ownership of source code and intellectual property to you upon completion.
