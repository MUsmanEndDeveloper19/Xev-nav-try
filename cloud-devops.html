Cloud & DevOps Questions Answered

Which cloud provider do you recommend — AWS, GCP, or Azure?

It depends on your existing stack, team expertise, and budget. We're certified across all three and recommend the provider that best fits your workload, compliance needs, and long-term cost structure after a technical audit.

How long does a cloud migration take?

A typical migration takes 4 to 10 weeks depending on infrastructure complexity and data volume. We use phased, zero-downtime migration strategies to minimize risk and business disruption.

Can you set up CI/CD for our existing codebase?

Yes. We integrate automated CI/CD pipelines into your existing repositories and workflows, regardless of your current tech stack, with minimal disruption to your development team.

Do you provide ongoing infrastructure support after launch?

Yes. We offer 24/7 monitoring, incident response, and maintenance retainers to keep your infrastructure secure, optimized, and running reliably long after the initial setup.

How do you handle security and compliance requirements?

We follow security-first infrastructure practices including encrypted secrets management, least-privilege access, and audit-ready configurations aligned with SOC 2, GDPR, and PIPEDA requirements.
