Enterprise Software Questions Answered

How is enterprise software different from a regular business application?

Enterprise software is built for organizational complexity — multiple departments, strict compliance requirements, thousands of concurrent users, and deep integrations with legacy systems that a standard business app isn't architected to handle.

How long does an enterprise software project take?

Most enterprise platforms take 4 to 9 months depending on scope, number of integrations, and compliance requirements. We provide a detailed phased roadmap with milestones after the discovery phase.

Can you integrate with our existing ERP, CRM, or legacy systems?

Yes. We regularly build custom integrations with SAP, Oracle, Salesforce, and internal legacy systems, ensuring new software connects seamlessly with what you already run.

How do you ensure compliance with SOC 2, GDPR, or industry regulations?

We follow security-first development practices including encryption, audit logging, and role-based access from day one, and we work directly with your compliance team to meet SOC 2, GDPR, or industry-specific requirements.

Do you provide dedicated project management for enterprise projects?

Yes. Every enterprise engagement includes a dedicated project manager, transparent Agile workflows, and regular stakeholder reporting throughout the project lifecycle.
