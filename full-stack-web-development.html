Web Development Questions Answered

What technologies do you use for web development?

We use modern technologies including React, Node.js, and MongoDB for full-stack development.

Our frontend stack includes HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and Tailwind CSS for responsive design.

For backend we rely on Express.js, REST APIs, and cloud platforms like AWS and Vercel.

How long does a web development project take?

A simple website typically takes 2–4 weeks depending on design complexity and content.

Medium-sized web applications with custom features usually take 6–10 weeks to complete.

Large enterprise-level projects can take 3–6 months including planning, development, and testing.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance after launch?

Yes, we offer monthly maintenance plans that include bug fixes, updates, and performance monitoring.

Our support team is available 24/7 to handle any critical issues that arise post-launch.

We also provide regular security patches and feature enhancements as part of our retainer packages.

Can you modernize or scale my existing application?

Absolutely, we specialize in legacy application modernization and technology stack upgrades.

We assess your current codebase and propose a phased migration plan to minimize downtime.

Our team ensures backward compatibility and smooth data migration throughout the process.

What does your development process look like?

We start with a discovery phase to understand your goals, requirements, and target audience.

Then we move into design, development, and testing sprints with regular client check-ins.

Finally, we handle deployment, handover, and provide documentation for your team.
